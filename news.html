March 11, 2026 Intellectual Property

Patent Filed for Gadolinium-Free Contrast MRI Synthesis

Pixelence has officially filed a patent for an AI system that generates brain contrast-enhanced MRI images without gadolinium contrast agents. The technology combines multi-sequence non-contrast MRI, deep pathology segmentation, and conditional generative models to create synthetic contrast — prioritising accuracy in pathology-positive regions.

This milestone brings us closer to making neuro-oncology imaging safer, faster, and more accessible for patients and hospitals where contrast administration poses risks or logistical challenges.

June 2024 Product

BrainEnhance Technology Officially Introduced

Pixelence publicly introduced BrainEnhance — our AI software that generates synthetic contrast-enhanced MRI scans from native non-contrast sequences using a generative U-Net architecture with attention blocks. The technology reduces imaging costs by 30%, enhances patient safety by eliminating gadolinium contrast agent risks, and enables more frequent monitoring for patients needing regular scans.
